College History

Govt. Science College

Government Science College (GSC) is a public educational institution located at Tejgaon in Dhaka. It is one of the two public colleges in Dhaka. It was formerly named Government Intermediate Technical College. It has Garden and individual Lab. This was the only college of Dhaka where science, technology, and basic engineering design could be studied throughout the High School level for the beginners with high ambitious to be an engineer or technologist/scientist.
